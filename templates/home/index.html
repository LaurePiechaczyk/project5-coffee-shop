From around the world for your favorite taste in your cup.

Our coffee comes from South America and Africa and we offer 2 different varieties, Arabica and Robusta, as well as blends of these.

The two varieties differ in taste and growing conditions. Arabica beans tend to have a milder, sweeter taste, with notes of sugar, fruit and berries. Robusta, on the other hand, has a stronger, harsher taste, with a grain undertone and a peanutty aftertaste. It contains twice as much caffeine as Arabica beans.
